BEST KANYE WEST SONGS OFF EACH ALBUM

The College Dropout

Family Business

This song is an emotional moment of peace while gazing off towards a sunset on the lake. An absolute classic.

Honorable Mention: All Falls Down

One of the go-to sing along songs, personally. It has an incredible feature, and I love old-school Kanye flow.

Late Registration

Hey Mama

UHHHH okay one of the top five BEST Kanye songs of all time. Incredible. Showstopping. Never been done before. Gets me all up in my feels.

Graduation

Everything I Am

Okay okay... just ~vibes~ here. Classic Kanye flow. Solid, but simple production. A beautifully bare track in Kanye's repertoire.

Honorable Mention: Good Life

Windows down. Driving down the highway. The overwhelming feeling of content swelling up inside of you. Just *chef's kiss*

808s & Heartbreaks

Coldest Winter

Probably my hottest of takes thus far, which is ironic given the name. The lyrics. The build in production. But mainly, the lyrics. Context: this song was written for his late mother. It hurts.

My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy

THE WHOLE ALBUM

Mr. Kanye West absolutely did NOT have to go this hard for us. But he did. And for that, I am thankful. This album is HOT FIRE FLAMES all around. You are lying to yourself if you think this isn't his most consistent, well-done piece of discography overall.

Watch the Throne

Ni**as in Paris

I literally do not even have to explain this one. If you know, you know. (p.s. What'd she order?)

Good Music Cruel Summer

To The World

A spiritual awakening. Truly. It's just a good song. Trust me.

Honorable Mention: Mercy

Bro, if you need some bad b energy in your life... this is it.

Yeezus

Bound 2

Another one in the top five all-times. The verses in this song... legendary.

Honorable Mention: Blood On The Leaves

This one many people disagree with me on. I do not know why I go so hard for this song. But wow. It is my anthem. A phenomenal workout song if you need that extra push in the last set.

The Life of Pablo

Waves

Least controversial pick by far. This is good music. Nobody can disagree.

Ye

Violent Crimes

This album was not it for me. But this song changes my life everytime I listen to it. A cultural reset.

Kids See Ghosts

Bye. No thanks.

Jesus is King

Use This Gospel

Not most people's favorite album. But I kinda liked it... Plus the Kenny G feature is fuego.
